Welcome to MailScribe!

Mailscribe is your hassle-free solution for swift and effortless email composition! Tired of the tedious process of logging into Gmail and navigating to the compose section every time you need to send a crucial email? Mailscribe is here to simplify your workflow. With this browser extension, you can effortlessly compose and send important emails directly without the need for repetitive logins and clicks.


You can find more about the developer and explore the project on GitHub. Upgrade your email experience with Mailscribe today!
